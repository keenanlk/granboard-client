Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

Overview

NLC Darts is a dart scoring app that connects to GranBoard dartboards via Bluetooth. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

NLC Darts does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included.
  • No data is sent to any server.

Local Data

Game scores and settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth to communicate with your GranBoard dartboard. Bluetooth is used solely for receiving dart hit events and controlling board LEDs. No data from Bluetooth connections is collected or transmitted.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
